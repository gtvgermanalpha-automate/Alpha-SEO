Pillar 01 Technical SEO & Performance

Fixing the hidden technical issues that hold your site back from ranking.

The engineering layer of SEO. If search engines can't crawl, render, or trust your pages, no amount of content will save you — so this is where we start.

Overview

What technical SEO actually is — and why it decides everything else.

Technical SEO is the engineering work that decides whether your content ever has a chance to rank. Crawlability, indexation, schema, rendering, performance, internal linking, hreflang, migration safety — the unsexy infrastructure layer most agencies skip.

We start every engagement with a forensic audit. Then we prioritise by impact, ship fixes in tight sprints, and lock everything in with CI guardrails so it doesn't regress next quarter.

What's included

Every technical engagement covers eight layers.

200+ Point Site Audit

Crawl-level diagnostic covering technical health, on-page coverage, content depth and backlink profile — delivered with a prioritised fix roadmap.

Core Web Vitals (LCP, INP, CLS)

Field-data analysis, real interaction profiling, fix deployment and Lighthouse CI guardrails so performance doesn't regress in production.

Schema Markup & Structured Data

JSON-LD graphs (Organization, Product, Article, FAQ, BreadcrumbList) deployed and validated. Rich-result eligibility tracked in Search Console.

Site Architecture & Internal Linking

Crawlable URL structure, contextual internal linking graph, breadcrumbs, faceted navigation rules, pagination strategy.

Crawl Budget & Indexation

Log-file analysis, robots directives, canonical consolidation, XML sitemap engineering — reclaim crawl budget wasted on parametrised URLs.

JavaScript SEO & Rendering

SSR / hybrid SSR audits, hydration analysis, validating that critical content reaches the rendered HTML — not just the client DOM.

Migration Safety

Pre-migration crawls, redirect mapping, schema parity, rollback playbooks — migrate platforms without losing a single ranking.

International & hreflang

Multi-market URL architecture, correct hreflang clusters, geo-targeting, localised content alignment — clean signals for every region.

How it works

Our technical engagement cadence.

01

Crawl & Diagnose

Full crawl using Screaming Frog + Sitebulb. Log-file analysis. Core Web Vitals field data. Schema validation. Output: a 30–40 page diagnostic document.

02

Prioritise by Impact

Every finding scored by impact, effort and confidence. Stack-ranked into a 90-day roadmap your team can actually execute against.

03

Implement in Sprints

Two-week sprints. Weekly check-ins. We ship the fixes ourselves, or hand off engineering-grade tickets to your team. Either way, things actually ship.

04

Verify & Lock In

Lighthouse CI guardrails, performance budgets, schema validation in deploys. So your gains don't quietly disappear next quarter.

What you receive

Concrete deliverables.

  • Comprehensive technical audit document (30–40 page PDF + live doc)
  • Prioritised 90-day fix roadmap (Linear / Jira / Notion — your tool)
  • Implementation by our team, OR engineering-grade tickets handed off to yours
  • Lighthouse CI configuration committed to your repo
  • Schema JSON-LD templates ready for production
  • Crawl-budget & indexation monitoring dashboard
  • Monthly executive readout with before / after metrics
  • Sister-engagement bundle option with our SEO content team
